HACKATHONS

Boka

Boka is an Android application that allows users to scan (or manually enter) a book's ISBN. The app then searches the web for the safest magnet/torrent/direct download links, and returns the best choices to the user. Awarded Best Mobile Hack at Hack.UVA (University of Virginia)

Givr

Givr is a web application that encourages charity donations. It will round up your transactions on your bank account to the nearest dollar and donate that amount to a charity of your choice. Awarded Grand Prize Hack at HACCK (University of Virginia)

Huddle

Huddle lets users post their study location on an interactive campus map, facilitating studying with partners and encouraging study sessions. Awarded 3rd place Hack at TribeHacks (College of William & Mary)

BitSplit

BitSplit will detect if the site will take bitcoin and then give you the QR code of the wallet, allowing quick transfers. Donate bitcoin easily to your favorite websites!
